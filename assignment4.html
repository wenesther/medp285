ASMR stands for "Autonomous Sensory Meridian Response" and has become popular all around the world.
The Google definition is: a feeling of well-being combined with a tingling sensation in the scalp and down the back of the neck, as experienced by some people in response to a specific gentle stimulus, often a particular sound.

Although NYC is currently going through Phase 1 of opening, social distancing is still encouraged. For those who either can't or don't want to go outside, but also don't want to miss summer, I've compiled some summer ASMR sounds to pair with the weekly weather report to simulate the experience of being outdoors.

For the assignment, I nested a grid within a grid. The audio section of this page is in a separate grid inside the page grid.
