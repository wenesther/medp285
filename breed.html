PUGS? WHAT ARE THEY?

This fun, little breed orginated from China, where they used to hide in the Emperor's sleeves and jump out at the necks of his enemies.
They're the largest breed within the toy category, making them the biggest small dogs.

Double-click the different body parts to learn about some of their physical traits!

round, wrinkly, flat face
(some of them have a black mask)

weirdly velvety ears

big, bulging eyes

soft, yet firm, compact body

curly tail

zoomie launchers



🙂 PROS AND CONS 🙁


🙂 excellent lap dogs

🙂 never picky with food

🙂 is only as active as you are

🙂 always excited and friendly

🙂 highly food motivated, making them easy to train

🙂 short hair means no grooming

🙁 does not understand the concept of personal space

🙁 will eat absolutely anything

🙁 prone to health issues due to their build

🙁 sensitive to extreme temperatures

🙁 loud snoring

🙁 shedding, shedding, and more shedding

For more information, visit The American Kennel Club , dogtime , and Pet hooligans.

note: As a pug owner, I find them to be very low maintenance, making them the perfect first dog for those who are looking to take care of one. Though they have their cons, it eventually becomes endearing and I wouldn't exchange them for anything.
